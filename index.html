Quest 4: Types of Inequality

4

Inequality shows up in many different ways. Each type can create barriers that make the journey harder for certain people.

Racial Inequality

When people are treated unfairly or have fewer opportunities because of their race or the color of their skin.

Gender Inequality

When people are treated unfairly or have fewer opportunities because of their gender or gender identity.

Economic Inequality

When some people have a lot of money and resources while others struggle to meet their basic needs.

Education Inequality

When some people have access to better schools, resources, and learning opportunities than others.

Health Inequality

When some people have better access to healthcare, medicine, and healthy living conditions than others.
